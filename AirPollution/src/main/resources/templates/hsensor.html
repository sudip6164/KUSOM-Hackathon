Detailed Information on Sound Pollution

Sound pollution, or noise pollution, can have detrimental effects on health and well-being. It can lead to stress, sleep disturbances, and other health issues.

What Contributes to Sound Pollution?

Various sources contribute to sound pollution, including traffic noise, industrial activities, construction sites, and noisy equipment.

Health Impacts of Sound Pollution

Long-term exposure to high levels of noise can result in hearing loss and other auditory problems. It can also contribute to cardiovascular diseases and psychological issues.
